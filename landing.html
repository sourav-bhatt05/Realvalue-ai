How it works

Three steps to smarter investing

No sign-ups, no credit cards. Just pick a spot on the map, fill in the details, and let our AI do the rest.

1

Select a Location

Click anywhere on the interactive Delhi NCR map, or search for a specific locality. The location auto-fills coordinates and the nearest address.

2

Enter Property Details

Specify BHK, area, furnishing, property type, and status. The area auto-fills based on BHK selection — but you can adjust it anytime.

3

Get AI Predictions

Hit "Analyze" and receive instant price predictions, rental estimates, investment scores, and AI-generated insights — all in real time.
